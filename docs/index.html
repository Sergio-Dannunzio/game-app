FACE THE WRATH OF GOD

33 Immortals is a co-op action-roguelike for 33 players. Play a damned soul, and rebel against God’s final judgment. Pick-up and raid, cooperate to survive hordes of monsters, defeat massive bosses, and face the wrath of God in a fight for your eternal life.

ABOUT 33 INMORTALS

Join the rebellion

add

Your mortal life has been judged sinful, and your soul’s fate is to be punished for all eternity – but an uprising is brewing among the damned! Press on as a renegade in the immortal rebellion against God’s final judgment, and fight for your eternal life.

Pick-up and raid

add

Jump start your rebellion – dive straight into epic co-op battles with instant matchmaking. Fight through a lean, mean distillation of the MMO raid experience.

33-player co-op

add

Find true strength in numbers. Optimize your skills and tactics to bolster your 33-player team, and tip the odds in your favor through powerful cooperative moves. Sustain your numbers as best you can, for only the strongest group of survivors stand a fighting chance in brutal boss battles.

Rise above the Almighty

add

Face the Wrath of God, and stay bold – for He will end your quest swiftly and often. Expand your epic arsenal with weapons empowered by sins and virtues, loot the spoils of His realms, equip potent new relics to match your playstyle, and gain permanent upgrades to your soul.

Why Early Access

add

We’re excited to continue Thunder Lotus’ proud and positive tradition of working with our community of players during game development. We’re off to a great start, but constructive player feedback is the key to bringing the game to the next level. Early Access lets players participate in development, and helps us explore different game ideas together with a live community to find what works best. We want to learn how you play the game and use that insight to make sure that 33 Immortals is a game you truly love.

What is a closed beta and how do i join

add

This is an early stage of development where we’re inviting members of our community to playtest the game, and tell us what they think! Our closed beta will be available on Epic Games Store and on the Xbox Game Pass. It will begin on May 24th and last until June 2nd 2024. Sign up to our mailing list to get a chance to play!

Where/when can i play?

add

The game will be available in Early Access in 2024 on Epic Games Store, Xbox Series X|S, Windows PC and with Xbox Game Pass for console and PC.

How are you planning on involving the Community?

add

We’re inviting the community to be the first to play 33 Immortals during the private testing phase of the project, and to share their constructive feedback. Please sign up in the form below if you’re interested – we hope you’ll consider taking part in the Early Access phase too! Meanwhile, joining the community in our Discord and helping it be a swell, positive place to be is always helpful.

How do i get in touch with the team?

add

The easiest way to get in touch with us is through Discord. We’re most responsive there, and thanks for your patience: we keep regular office hours in the Eastern Time Zone.
